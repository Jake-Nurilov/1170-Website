Welcome to my portfolio!

Hi, I'm Jake Nurilov, I am a first year at Dalhousie University and I study Computer Science.
I have first started coding in grade 7 using online courses, but I truly fell in love with coding in grade 9 when I joined my school's coding club, and have been coding ever since.
I like making small games that I find enjoyable to code and play, and I make them from scratch, usually in python using the turtle graphics library.
The reason why I use turtle and not something like Pygame is because I have to make everything from the ground up, and there are no "Black Boxes" that take care of things for you.
The games I make are ususally either original games that I come up with or my own versions of old classic games with a little twist added to them.
