About the game:

In this game you play as the Skelly Slayer and are trapped in a dungeon.
You have to fight off hordes of skeletons and survive for as long as you can for a high score!
You gain points for your score by:

  • Killing skeletons
  • Surviving for long
  • Picking up EXP orbs

The game has an EXP System which is broken down in the table below the video, and has a health system.
As the game progresses and you gain new skills and abilities, the amount of skeletons also increase.
